What is Computing?

Computing, in the context of computer science, refers to the process of utilizing computer systems to solve problems. This involves the design and use of algorithms to manipulate, process, and store digital information. It encompasses a wide range of disciplines, including software development, data analysis, artificial intelligence, and more.
